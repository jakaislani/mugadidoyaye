Devraj Patel Accident, Parents Mother Gauri Patel And Father

Devraj Patel Accident has left the Indian Community grieving and searching for answers. A devastating incident that claimed the life of the talented young comedian.

Tragedy shook the Indian community when 22-year-old Devraj Patel, a promising young YouTuber and comedian, died in a tragic accident.

Devraj Patel was born in 2001 in Mahasamud District, Chhattisgarh. He was initially from Daab Pali village, where his entire family still resides.

In 2020, he launched the “Dil Se Bura Lagta Hai – Devraj Patel Official” YouTube channel, which has 4.5 million followers as of 2023.

Patel was pursuing Graduation and lived in Korba City in 2021. Later, he relocated to Raipur to pursue his love for content creation.

Moreover, The idea of creating different comedy videos, memes, and reels helped him win the affection and support of his supporters.

Devraj Patel Accident

Devraj Patel’s Accident took place on Tuesday, June 26, 2023. The death news of a young YouTuber and comedian shattered the entire country.

The Tragedy happened at 3:30 pm when Devraj and his buddy were riding their bikes back from Nava Raipur after recording a short film.

Devraj sat behind his friend, who was riding the bike. The accident occurred when a speeding vehicle traveling in the same direction collided with the bike’s handle.

Devraj was hit by the truck’s large tire, injuring his friend, and was immediately transported to the hospital, where he was declared dead.

Investigations indicate that the truck driver drove carelessly and collided with Devraj’s bike.

When his death became public, his followers from all across the country expressed their sorrow by posting many memes and reels of the young viral celebrity.

Likewise, Chhattisgarh Chief Minister Bhupesh Bhagel uploaded an old video of Devraj on his Twitter account and expressed sadness.

The CM also mentioned that Devraj Patel, who created a name for himself with ‘Dil Se Bura Lagta Hai’ and made us all laugh died today.

Furthermore, he added, Losing such a talented young person is heartbreaking. May God grant his family and loved ones the courage to face this loss.

The death of Devraj Patel is a devastating blow to the Indian YouTube community and the entertainment industry.

YouTuber Patel Parents

Devraj Patel originates from a loving and tightly connected family.

Patel’s parents, Ghanshyam Patel, his father and Gauri Patel, his mother, have always supported his life.

His Father was a farmer, while his mother was a housewife. 

Before traveling to Raipur, he lived with his parents in Mahasamud District, Chhattisgarh.

He also has a brother, Hemant Patel and a sister whose identity remains unknown.

Devraj’s parents have played essential roles in his development, offering direction, love, and support while he followed his goals and ambitions.

Although born and grew up in a middle-class family, His parents have provided a caring and supportive atmosphere for Devraj to flourish.

Devraj Patel’s accident has undoubtedly had a significant effect on his family.

They are most likely surrounded by the affection and support of their close friends and families as they navigate this challenging period.

The outpouring of sorrow and love from his followers and community serves as a reminder of his influence on many people’s lives.
