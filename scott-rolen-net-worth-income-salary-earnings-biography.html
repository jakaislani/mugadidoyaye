Scott Rolen Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Bruce Rolen (born April 4, 1975) is a former professional baseball third baseman in Major League Baseball who played for the Los Angeles Dodgers (MLB). He was a member of the Philadelphia Phillies, St. Louis Cardinals, Toronto Blue Jays, and Cincinnati Reds, among other organisations. He was a seven-time All-Star and an eight-time Gold Glove winner throughout his career.
