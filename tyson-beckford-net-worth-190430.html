Tyson Beckford Net Worth

Tyson Beckford is an American actor and former model born on December 19, 1970 in Rochester, NY. He is best known for his work as a model for Ralph Lauren Polo and as the host of Bravo's Make Me a Supermodel. He has also appeared in films such as Into the Blue and Kings of the Evening.
