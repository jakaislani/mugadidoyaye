Miranda Lambert Gets a Fresh Cut, Goes Without Makeup

Here's reason number 8,961 why we love Miranda Lambert: She isn't above posting a makeup-free pic on her Instagram account. 

You might associate the 30-year-old country star with her red carpet style: big hair, all done up, blinged out jewelry—but she looks just as fabulous when she doesn't have a stitch of makeup on.

Miranda took some downtime during her summer tour to get a fresh new cut and color boost, and then posted the pic—makeup-free—on her Instagram account on Tuesday.

"Fresh cut! Fresh color! 'Somethin' bout PLATINUM! Thanks @amandacraig12! Love you," she captioned the photo, giving her hairstylist a deserved shout-out.

In the pic Miranda poses with Amanda wearing a gray tank, gold necklace and absolutely no makeup. If only we woke up like this!

Miranda went for the plunge and chopped off her long hair back in June, opting for a bob. Although, since she's sometimes added in extensions when she felt like going long again. Now she's cropped it just a tad shorter, with some choppy layers for extra dimension.

We love this down-to-earth look on Miranda! What do you think of Miranda's short and sweet cut—and her makeup-free pic?
