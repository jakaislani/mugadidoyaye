News: Phase 2 Runes bring fresh abilities to the game while also establishing designated slots for players to harness these powers. Although the precise information regarding these slots remains ambiguous, it is confirmed that Runes will be compatible with belts and boots. This paves the way for players to personalize their characters and elevate their gaming experience with a myriad of options.

Discover New Class Abilities

Each class in WoW Classic receives two new abilities through Phase 2 Runes. Let’s take a closer look at what each class gains:

1. Druids

Eclipse amplifies the potency of the Wrath spell, empowering the wielder to inflict greater damage. Tiger’s Fury supplements this effect by providing an extra surge of energy, enabling the user to execute even more formidable attacks.

2. Paladins

Sheath of Light – This ability increases the amount of healing they receive from critical heals, making them more effective healers. Guarded by the Light helps them regenerate mana, ensuring they can sustain their healing abilities.

3. Hunters

Melee Specialist augments the viability of melee hunting by boosting the effectiveness of their close-range attacks. Furthermore, Trap Launcher enables them to deploy traps from a distance, providing enhanced control over their environment.

4. Warriors

Rallying Cry – This ability boosts the health of party members, providing valuable support during intense battles. Blood Surge makes certain attacks instant and cost no Rage, allowing warriors to unleash a flurry of devastating strikes.

5. Rogues

Shuriken Toss inflicts damage while bestowing combo points, granting rogues versatile combat capabilities. Master of Subtlety further enhances their damage post-stealth, transforming them into even deadlier assassins.

6. Priests

Mind Spike – This ability deals damage and increases the chance of critical hits, making priests formidable damage dealers. Pain Suppression reduces the damage they take, increasing their survivability in challenging encounters.

7. Warlocks

Invocation – This ability deals instant damage when refreshing certain spells, adding an extra punch to their spellcasting arsenal. Dance of the Wicked boosts their dodge chance and mana, making them harder to hit and more efficient in battles.

8. Mages

Missile Barrage – This ability reduces the duration and cost of certain spells, allowing mages to unleash their magical prowess more frequently. Chronostatic Preservation stores healing energy, ensuring mages can sustain themselves during prolonged engagements.

9. Shamans

Maelstrom Weapon diminishes spell cast time, allowing shamans to expedite the release of their spells. Spirit of the Alpha heightens their threat, enhancing their role as formidable tanks. Meanwhile, Two-Handed Mastery increases attack speed when wielding two-handed weapons.

Prepare for the Season of Discovery

Commencing on November 30th, the WoW Classic Season of Discovery introduces thrilling enhancements to the initial 25 levels of the game. As players advance, they encounter fresh challenges and experiences, including a revamped iteration of the Blackfathom Deeps raid that enables 10 players to collaborate in defeating formidable bosses.

Moreover, engaging PvP events offer players the opportunity to showcase their skills in clashes within Ashenvale. Right from the onset of the season, participants can partake in Battlegrounds and the Honor system, injecting a heightened sense of competition into their gameplay.

In the second phase of the Season of Discovery in WoW Classic, the introduction of Runes adds a dynamic element by providing new class abilities and elevating gameplay for all players. Infused with distinctive powers and customizable features, Runes contribute an additional layer of excitement and depth to the overall gaming experience. Whether you align with the Druid, Paladin, Hunter, Warrior, Rogue, Priest, Warlock, Mage, or Shaman class, delving into the opportunities presented by Phase 2 Runes is essential. Embark on the Season of Discovery and unleash the latent power within!
