8 Things You Didnt Know About Ushna Shah

➡ Ushna Shah Biography

Ushna Shah, a well-known actress, works in the Pakistani entertainment industry and established herself as the best actress. She has worked in many top-ranked television serials including ‘Alif Allah Aur Insan’, and others that are worth watching. Also, she has modeled for various top-notched Pakistani brands. Alongside, she made a presence in some Pakistani films including ‘Bashar Momin’ a thriller released in 2014. She got huge applause and much appreciation for giving brilliant performances during her career life. However, here we have some more information you probably did not about. So, let us have a look!
