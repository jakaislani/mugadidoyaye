Asa Butterfield is an uprising English actor. He initiated his career in acting when he was only 9 years old with television series “After Thomas” as well as the comedy movie Son of Rambow. Asa Butterfield became recognized for portraying the leading character Bruno in the Holocaust movie The Boy in Striped Pyjamas, for which he got nominations for British Independent Filmfare Award and London Critics Circle Award for Youngest British Actor of the Year at 11 years of age of. He also portrayed the role of young Mordred in BBC television series Merlin and Norman in the fantasy movie Big Bang and Nanny McPhee.

For his breathtaking role of Hugo Cabret in Martin Scorsese’s series Hugo, Asa Butterfield received immense praise and was bestowed with Young Hollywood Award for remarkable Performance Male and was elected for the Critics’ Choice Film Award for Best Young Actor as well as the Empire Award for the Best Male Newcomer, amongst other awards. Asa is also recognized for depicting the role of Ender Wiggin in 2013 movie adaptation of fiction novel Ender’s Game. He was also elected for a British Independent Movie Award for the Best Actor for representing the role of Nathan Ellis in Morgan Matthews’s X+Y. He appeared as Jacob “Jake” in Miss Peregrine’s Home for Peculiar Children and as Gardner in The Space Between Us. In the year 2019, Butterfield appeared as Otis Milburn in Netflix comic series Sex Education.

Asa Butterfield was born in Islington, London, United Kingdom on 1st April, 197. He is the son of Sam Butterfield and Jacqueline Farr. He was named as “Asa Thornton Farr Butterfield” at birth, though now call by name “Asa Bopp Farr Butterfield” on his passport instead, taking “Bopp” as a mid-name after Comet Hale–Bopp, which passed conjugation on his birth day.

Asa Butterfield enjoys composing music, and released a songs mash up “Making Plans For Nigel” by XTC and “Teenage Dirtbag” by Wheatus. In the year 2012, Asa Butterfield co-created a turn-based audiovisual game for iPad with his brother and father called Racing Blind. On 7th April, 2013, this game was released on App Store.

Asa Butterfield is fascinated towards competitive Nintendo games. In the year 2017, he took part in Nintendo Worldwide Championships, in which he was eliminated earlier in invitational. Asa is a passionate player of Super Smash Bros. and has also signed with eSports Panda Global team under the code “Stimpy”. His first entrance with the organization was at 6 Genesis.
