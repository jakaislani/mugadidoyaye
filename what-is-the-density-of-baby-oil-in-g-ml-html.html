What method do you use to determine the density of oil?

Weigh the flask in which the oil is contained. Subtract the mass of the flask from the total mass of the oil and you will obtain the mass of 1 litre of oil. Finally, take the mass of the oil and divide it by the volume of the oil to get the density.
