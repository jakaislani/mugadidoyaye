Biography

Philip Zimbardo was born in New York on March 23, 1933. Psychologist and Stanford University professor who gained fame in the science community for his Stanford prison experiment. This experiment proved that abusive behavior in prison was not caused by inherent personality traits of prisoners and guards but by situational conditions. He and Paul Erlich both became professors at Stanford University. He earned a Ph.D. from Yale University after graduating summa cum ladue from Brooklyn College in 1954.
