Mitch Marner Net Worth

Mitch Marner is a Canadian forward who was born on May 5, 1997 in Markham, Canada. He was drafted fourth overall by the Toronto Maple Leafs in the 2015 NHL Draft after playing two seasons with the OHL's London Knights. Marner has since become a key player for the Maple Leafs and is considered one of the best young players in the NHL.
