Can you bake metal in polymer clay?

published on July 30, 2024
Metal of any kind is absolutely no problem to bake with polymer clay (or with liquid polymer clay). Can you bake polymer clay with wire in it? With polymer clay, the baking temp is a low 265 degrees F and will not melt wire. Therefore you can use any wire you like baked inside your clay.
